I Went Speed-Dating in Tokyo: It Explains Japan’s Sexless Population Explosion!

Exclusive conversations with a bevy of beautiful, single girls? What could go wrong…

“10 sexy Japanese girls, 2 good-looking Western guys, and 8 absolute chumps. I’m tellin’ ya bro, we can’t lose.”

That was how my friend, Dante, tried to sell me on the idea of speed-dating in Tokyo. I was newly single, gagging to mingle, and I liked the odds he’d put in front of me.

10 sexy Japanese girls? It was 10 x Yes for me — a Yes-tival!

After recently ending a 3-year relationship, I was up for some fun. And summer in Tokyo? That meant short skirts, low-cut tops, cold beers, and long nights under neon lights.

Sold!

When the day arrived, I wasn’t sure what to wear. Too casual, and I’d be signalling that the train fare home might be a struggle, let alone dinner for two at the Hyatt Hotel rooftop bar.

Too formal, and the girls might think I had the engagement ring burning a yearning hole in my desperate pocket!

I opted for smart-casual with some check Vans slip-ons and hoped my tanned skin and blond, sun-bleached hair would do most of the hard work for me.

By the time we got to the bar in Roppongi, I’d already downed two beers on the train ride and was dying for another.

Not so fast.

First came the name check, registration papers, payment, number card, pencil, eraser, notepad, and rules explanation.

They weren’t making this easy!

At least I got a free drink voucher.

I thought I’d use my freebie to flirt with the few girls who’d arrived early. Like every other guy standing around trying to look disinterested and aloof, I’d quickly scanned the ladies the moment I’d entered.

I’d like to say I hadn’t, but this was speed-dating, and I was only getting 5 or so…
